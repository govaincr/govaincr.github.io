About us

Who are we?

Foody is a restaurant that focuses on delivering great and tasty food without putting your health and well-being on the line.

Our story

Foody was created in may 2019, in a small location in Brooklyn, always with the same goal, deliver good food without riskins our client's health. Because of that, our customers have always been loyal, despite being small in numbers back then. After a year of hard work, we had enough ressources to expend into our current location, where we've been for 2 years, we've had more customers and hired more staff, always selecting the best.
